Classification of Abnormalities in ECG using Machine Learning

Aravintha Prasath V, et al.

Journal of Healthcare Engineering

May 2021

IoT ECG MSVM

Proposed an IoT-based health monitoring system for ambulatory ECG recorders using Heart Rate Variability (HRV) and a Modified Support Vector Machine (MSVM) algorithm. The system effectively detects cardiac abnormalities from ECG signals while addressing challenges like memory, battery, and processing load.
